The Resurgence of Yellow Gold

Why traditional hues are dominating modern collections...

 A. Smith Published Oct 15, 2023
Words: 432

The evolution of luxury jewellery is subtle, often moving in cycles rather than dramatic shifts. As we move through 2024, the demand for unique, personalized cuts is overshadowing traditional brilliant rounds.

The Rise of the Oval

Elongated shapes, particularly oval and radiant cuts, provide a larger surface area relative to carat weight. This creates an illusion of greater size while offering a flattering profile on the hand.

  • Increased perceived size per carat.
  • Flattering elongation of the finger.
  • Versatility in both vintage and modern settings.
